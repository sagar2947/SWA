Guiding Studens regarding trafic rules

Had one-to-one conversations with students to help them understand traffic rules and raise awareness about traffic regulations. The session included interactive discussions, practical demonstrations, and distribution of informative pamphlets. Students were encouraged to ask questions and share their experiences, making the session highly engaging and informative. The goal was to instill a sense of responsibility and awareness about the importance of following traffic rules for their safety and the safety of others.
